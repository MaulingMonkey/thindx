fn on_voice_error(&self, buffer_context: &Self::BufferContext, error: HResult)

[microsoft.com] Called in the event of a critical error during voice processing, such as a failing xAPO or an error from the hardware XMA decoder. The voice may have to be destroyed and re-created to recover from the error. The callback arguments report which buffer was being processed when the error occurred, and its HRESULT code.
